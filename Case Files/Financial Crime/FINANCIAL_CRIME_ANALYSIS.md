## SECTION 5: CRIMINAL LIABILITY ANALYSIS

### Securities Fraud (15 U.S.C. § 77e, § 78j)

**Elements:**
1. Operating as venture capitalist WITHOUT SEC registration
2. Raising capital from investors (directly or via funds)
3. Failure to register as investment adviser (if managing funds)
4. Disclosure violations (not revealing trafficking source of capital)

**Liability:**
- Epstein: Criminal + Civil (SEC can pursue posthumously against estate)
- Intermediaries (Grosof, Nikolic, Bucher, etc.): Joint liability
- Penalty: Fines $5M–$50M+ (civil); 5–10 years federal prison (criminal)

### Money Laundering (18 U.S.C. § 1956, § 1957)

**Elements:**
1. Knowing that property is proceeds of illegal activity (trafficking)
2. Engaging in financial transaction with intent to conceal/disguise
3. Transaction in interstate commerce / international finance

**Liability:**
- Epstein: 10–20 years federal prison (per count; stacked)
- Banks (Deutsche, JPM, Morgan Stanley): Fines $millions (AML failures)
- Intermediaries: Criminal liability (if knowing participation)

**Aggravating factor:**
- Trafficking proceeds (Category A felony) = enhanced penalties

### Technology Transfer / Export Control Violations (22 U.S.C. § 2778)

**Elements:**
1. Knowing transfer of controlled technology (BCI, AI, neurotechnology)
2. To foreign nationals / foreign states
3. Without State Department license/approval (ITAR/EAR)

**Liability:**
- Epstein: 20 years federal prison + $1M fine (per violation)
- Technology companies (Neuralink, Synchron, etc.): Liability if knowing (unlikely)

**Aggravating factor:**
- Transfer to adversary states (Russia) = enhanced penalties

### Foreign Agent Activities (18 U.S.C. § 951)

**Elements:**
1. Acting as agent of foreign government (Israel, Russia, Saudi Arabia)
2. Without prior notification to Attorney General
3. Engaging in political / intelligence activities on behalf of foreign state

**Liability:**
- Epstein: 10 years federal prison (if prosecutable posthumously)
- Intermediaries: Criminal liability (if acting as unregistered foreign agents)
